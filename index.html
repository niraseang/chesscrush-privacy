Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 12, 2026

ChessCrush ("the App") is a mobile game developed by Gunk Bug ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Locally Stored Data

The App stores game-progress data (such as level completion, scores, and settings) locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

1.2 Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and process certain data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy, including:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region level)
  • App usage and interaction data related to ads

We do not have access to personally identifiable information collected by AdMob.

1.3 Analytics

The App does not use any analytics or tracking services beyond what is provided by AdMob and the Google Play platform.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or other personal information.
  • We do not require account creation or login.
  • We do not access your contacts, camera, microphone, or location services.
  • We do not collect or store any data on our servers.

3. Children's Privacy

The App is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Ad content served by AdMob is configured to comply with applicable regulations. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google AdMob – Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Services – Privacy Policy

5. Data Retention and Deletion

All game data is stored locally on your device. You can delete it at any time by clearing the App's data in your device settings or by uninstalling the App.

6. Your Consent

By using the App, you consent to this Privacy Policy. Where required by law (e.g., GDPR, CCPA), a consent dialog will be presented before personalized ads are shown.
