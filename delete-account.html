ChessCrush Account Deletion

ChessCrush does not keep a standalone account database on our servers. Core game progress is stored locally on your device.

How to delete your data

  1. Delete local game data: Android Settings -> Apps -> ChessCrush -> Storage -> Clear Data, or uninstall the app.
  2. Remove Google linking (optional): Google Account Permissions, then remove ChessCrush / Google Play Games access.
